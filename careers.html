Careers

Build DroneCorp. with us

DroneCorp. is a very early-stage effort exploring U.S.-made unmanned systems and autonomy R&D. These roles are written for people who are comfortable in ambiguous, low-infrastructure environments and who want to help build something from almost zero.

Open roles
Head of Programs & Growth
Remote / U.S.-based · Defense & dual-use
Scaled defense work before · knows what “good” looks like
›

You’ve helped take at least one defense or dual-use startup from “a few prototypes” to meaningful programs with primes or government customers. You understand BD, capture, and execution, but you’re happy to work in the weeds with engineers and founders.

  • Shape early opportunity pipeline and help decide which programs we should chase or decline.
  • Translate messy customer problems into clear technical work packages and timelines.
  • Build lightweight program-management habits—rhythms, reporting, basic tools.
  • Act as a calm adult in the room when we’re dealing with new primes or government sponsors.
Founding Systems & Flight Engineer
Hands-on · Air, maritime, & interior UAS
Prototype-heavy · full-stack hardware / software
›

This is the “do everything technical” role alongside the founder—airframes, payloads, integration, bench testing, and early flight work. You are comfortable bouncing between CAD, wiring harnesses, ROS-style stacks, and field tests.

  • Own small-batch builds of concept platforms, from parts ordering to bench checkout.
  • Work with autonomy and perception code, even if you’re not a research scientist.
  • Instrument prototypes for data collection and flight-test reporting.
  • Help define what our first “real” engineering team should look like.
Studio & Vibe Operations Lead
Arizona-based · part-time / flexible
Snacks, soundtrack, back-of-house chaos wrangler
›

Lightweight, fun role for someone who treats a workshop like a backstage kitchen. You keep the lights on, gear where it belongs, and morale high. Think studio manager + kitchen lead + “is everyone drinking water?” checker.

  • Stock and run a tiny kitchen / snack bar for long build and test days.
  • Help keep tools, parts, and work areas sane without turning the place into a bureaucracy.
  • Coordinate little things that make the lab feel like a place people want to be.
  • Pitch in on shipping, receiving, and event days when we’re hosting visitors.
Apply or introduce yourself

There is no formal HR stack here yet. If one of these roles feels like you—or you think you’re adjacent and should be in the conversation—send a short note. Links to a resume, portfolio, or profile are more useful than long cover letters.

DroneCorp. is an early-stage effort. Some roles may begin as contract, advisory, or part-time engagements while we stand up real programs and revenue.
