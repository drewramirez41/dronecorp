Careers

Build DroneCorp. from the zero line

DroneCorp. is an early-stage U.S. unmanned systems effort focused on autonomy-heavy ISR concepts, small-batch manufacturing, and defense-aligned R&D. We’re not a scaled prime—we’re building toward that with a very small, high-trust team that wants to wire the first racks, write the first flight code, and sit in the first customer briefings.

Open roles

Early-stage hiring priorities

Founding Defense Programs & Manufacturing Lead
Defense | Capture Hybrid U.S.-based
›

Own the path from concept deck to first program-of-record. You’ve previously helped a hardware or defense startup move from prototypes into awarded contracts, and you’re comfortable living in the overlap between capture, manufacturing, and operations.

  • Stand up small-batch manufacturing and quality workflows suitable for defense work.
  • Shape capture strategy, teaming approaches, and proposal content.
  • Coordinate with engineering on costed BOMs, lead times, and delivery plans.
  • Comfort leading conversations with primes, integrators, and end-users.
Senior Autonomy & Systems Engineer
R&D Remote-friendly (US)
›

Help design the brains of the stack: onboard autonomy, edge processing, perception and navigation for contested environments. You’re comfortable going from whiteboard to working prototype, and you’re opinionated about architectures.

  • Own key autonomy or perception modules for interior, low-altitude, or maritime ISR.
  • Work across flight stack, simulation, and integration with C2 systems.
  • Partner with hardware to define compute, sensor, and networking requirements.
Prototype Operations & Flight Test Lead
Hands-on On-site leaning Arizona / Southwest
›

Run the practical side of the house: integration, ground testing, flight test events, and ops checklists. You’re comfortable in the field, you like checklists more than slideware, and you’re willing to help wire, solder, and carry cases.

  • Own test planning, range coordination, and safety practices.
  • Capture flight data and feedback into design and autonomy teams.
  • Help define how a future small operations team should run.
Studio Kitchen & Vibe Operations Lead
Culture On-site Tucson Area (future)
›

Early-stage teams run hot. This role keeps the back-of-house running and the front-of-house sane. You’ve run a kitchen, café, or event space before and understand how to keep snacks stocked, people fed, and the vibe steady.

  • Own the studio kitchen, snacks, and “we actually have coffee” reality.
  • Coordinate team meals, late-night work sessions, and visiting partner days.
  • Help with light operations tasks: shipping boxes, staging hardware, runs to the store.
