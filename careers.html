Careers

Build DroneCorp. from the zero line

DroneCorp. is an early-stage U.S. unmanned systems effort focused on autonomy-heavy ISR concepts, small-batch manufacturing, and defense-aligned R&D. We are not a scaled prime—we’re building toward that with a very small, high-trust team that wants to wire the first racks, write the first flight code, and sit in the first customer briefings.

If you’ve ever wanted to see a program mature from one-off prototypes to operational racks, this is the zero line. We care less about pedigree and more about ownership: people who will move fast, hold the standard, and speak plainly about what is or isn’t ready for the field.

Relevant backgrounds include UAS integration, RF / EW, embedded software, safety-critical systems, ground control stacks, mechanical design, and small-batch manufacturing. U.S. person status is required; prior clearance work is a plus but not a prerequisite.
