Platforms

Small-batch unmanned aerial systems

DroneCorp. platforms are designed around ISR and tactical robotics use-cases that favor reliability, modular payloads, and manufacturability inside the U.S. supply chain.

Short-range ISR quad

Rugged, man-portable ISR quad with swappable day / low-light payloads and encrypted digital link. Tuned for sub-10 km missions with fast launch and recover cycles.

Indoor recon stack

Collision-tolerant micro-UAS with onboard autonomy primitives for indoor and GPS-denied reconnaissance, paired with a simple handheld control interface.

Swarm rack concept

Concept work on vehicle- or ship-mounted racks of small UAS that can be queued, launched, recovered, and turned quickly with minimal crew burden.
