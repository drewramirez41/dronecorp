Platforms

ISR racks, not show pieces

The intent is to build tight families of ISR platforms that share components, logs, and behaviors. Names, sizes, and configurations may change; the underlying idea—sovereign, repeatable hardware—does not.

SR–01

Short-range ISR quad

Compact multirotor tuned for line-of-sight and near-beyond-line-of-sight work. Designed to launch quickly, survive rough recoveries, and hand back useful telemetry instead of pretty footage alone.

IN–01

Interior & shipboard recon

Prop-guarded, sensor-heavy variants biased toward interior, ship, and industrial spaces. Trade some endurance for reliability in GPS-denied, RF-hostile environments.

SW–RACK

Swarm & rack concepts

Exploratory work around dense racks of small air vehicles—simple, disposable units that matter more as a group than as individuals. Focus on launch/recover mechanics and telemetry.

All platform descriptions are conceptual and may change. Nothing here should be read as a production configuration or a commitment to field specific capabilities.
