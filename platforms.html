Scope

What DroneCorp. is exploring

This site describes where the work is heading, not a catalog of signed programs. The emphasis is on small, sharp platforms with honest constraints, built for teams that care about control loops more than PowerPoints.

ISR Platforms

Short-range autonomous ISR

Compact multirotors optimized for dense urban, maritime, and interior runs. Focus on low-friction deployment, strong telemetry, and recoverable logs instead of fragile one-off demos.

Manufacturing

Microfoundry mindset

U.S.-based builds, tight batches, and iterative hardware revisions. The goal is a steady cadence of testable hardware, not a single “big” production line that never changes.

Edge autonomy

Onboard decision-making

Experimentation with onboard perception, guidance, and simple autonomy stacks that survive RF-hostile, GPS-denied environments without needing a rack of cloud services to function.
