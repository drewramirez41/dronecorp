Design Focus
Applied unmanned systems R&D
Platforms described on this page are early-stage concepts. They are intended to explore autonomy frameworks, payload integration patterns, and edge-processing architectures across air, surface, and confined interior environments. Each concept is designed around a U.S.-centric supply chain, emphasizing reliability, maintainability, and clean interfaces to existing C2, mapping, and analytics toolchains.
