Concept Platforms Under Evaluation

DroneCorp. explores modular air, maritime, and interior-recon architectures designed to integrate with existing command-and-control ecosystems. Work is centered on design studies, prototyping pathways, and integration concepts.

Design Focus

Applied unmanned systems R&D

Early-stage exploration of autonomy frameworks, payload integration patterns, and edge-processing architectures across air, surface, and confined interior environments.

Platform Families

Tactical ISR Quadrotor

Multi-sensor close-range reconnaissance platform emphasizing low signature, edge-AI navigation helps reduce operator workload while maintaining human-on-the-loop authority.

Distributed Swarm Constructs

Research into cooperative mesh autonomy, resilient comms, graceful degradation, and integration with existing mission-planning tools rather than bespoke stovepipes.

Confined-Space Nano-UAS

Interior reconnaissance vehicle for sub-terrain and low-light conditions. Supports mapping, routing, and digital-twin generation in complex spaces.
