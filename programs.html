Internal efforts

Programs

DroneCorp. is not a prime; current work is organized into focused internal programs that explore specific pieces of the stack. Each effort is structured so it can plug into a larger customer program when the timing and partners are right.

Rack zero

Carrier-adjacent ISR line

Concept / lab

Early build-space for surface and short-range air platforms that live alongside carrier and pier-side operations. Focus on power, launch/ recovery, and data ingest workflows rather than cosmetics.

• Maritime perimeter ISR • Physical + simulated environments
Interior ISR

Confined-space autonomy rig

Active

Hardware-in-the-loop testbed for navigating industrial and sub-terranean environments. Uses configurable obstacles, lighting, and RF conditions to exercise perception, mapping, and comms strategies.

• GPS-denied navigation • Digital-twin data products
Swarm & teaming

Distributed autonomy experiments

Exploratory

Simulation-first work on small, cooperating vehicles that share sensing and routing decisions. Emphasis on open interfaces so frameworks can be swapped or extended without rewriting the entire stack.

• Mesh networking • Open-source-friendly interfaces
