Collaboration

Partner with DroneCorp.

DroneCorp. is positioned as a small, technically focused outfit that plugs into larger defense and research ecosystems. We are not a prime integrator; we’re a focused autonomy and concept-platform shop that plays well with others.

Defense primes & large integrators
Concept feeders, not competitors

Support early-stage autonomy concepts, platform studies, and risk-reduction work that can graduate into your capture and program portfolios. We focus on clean handoff documentation, integration hooks, and transparent technical assumptions so you can carry work forward at scale.

Government sponsors
Exploratory & applied R&D

Provide targeted experiments and prototyping against specific mission problems—especially where autonomy, interior recon, or maritime ISR are involved. Emphasis on data products, reports, and lessons learned that can inform future program language or larger acquisition efforts.

Specialized vendors & startups
Payloads, components, and stacks

Explore how your sensors, compute modules, radios, or autonomy software might be composed into small, tightly scoped unmanned platforms. DroneCorp. can act as a neutral integrator to show what’s possible before joint proposals or larger partnerships.

Universities & labs
Pathways to the field

Bridge promising research into practical demonstrators and fieldable prototypes. We focus on packaging, test design, and documentation that allows sponsored research to be evaluated in realistic environments with sponsor-friendly reporting.
